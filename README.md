# Digital Marketing in Aviation: Analysis of Airline Strategies in the UK & Ireland

A research and data analysis project focused on evaluating digital marketing strategies used by major airlines in the UK and Ireland as part of a Master’s at South East Technological University (SETU). The study was carried out using Python, with a focus on statistical analysis, exploratory data analysis, visualisations, and thematic analysis of survey and interview data to generate actionable insights and strategic recommendations for airline marketing.

## Methodology
### 1. Mixed-Methods Approach
The study used a combination of quantitative surveys and qualitative interviews to capture both numerical trends and user perceptions.

### 2. Quantitative research
208 survey responses collected via Google Forms using 17 structured questions distributed through email and WhatsApp.

### 3. Qualitative research
3 semi-structured interviews conducted with 2 digital marketing professionals and 1 frequent flyer.

## Data Collection
Google Forms, Email Surveys, WhatsApp 

## Tools & Technologies
Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy), Jupyter Notebook, Anaconda, Microsoft Excel, Google Forms, Power BI.

## Analysis Techniques
Statistical analysis, exploratory data analysis, data visualisation, and thematic analysis were used to interpret the data.

## Research Frameworks
PESTLE Analysis, Porter’s Five Forces, SWOT Analysis, and Thematic Analysis were applied to structure findings and evaluate the airline marketing landscape.

## Key Outcomes

The majority of respondents (79.8%) were aged 18–34, indicating strong representation of digitally active travelers. Airline websites and online travel agencies were identified as the most influential booking channels. Personalized marketing and real-time communication tools, such as chatbots, were found to significantly improve engagement, satisfaction, and booking intent, particularly among younger users. Loyalty program members also demonstrated higher engagement and increased travel frequency compared to non-members.

## Key Recommendation
Airlines should prioritize AI-driven personalization, real-time customer communication, and integrated omnichannel experiences to improve customer satisfaction, retention, and long-term competitive advantage.
